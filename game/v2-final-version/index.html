How to Play:

The goal of the game is to get as close to the sum of 21 as possible without busting it!

Each card value is represented by their number, except that the face cards all represent 10, and ace can be 1 or 11 depending on the score.

The randomly decided player to go first will add as many cards as they want with the goal in mind. When they are confident with their score, they can pass the turn onto the next player.

After the second player is confident with their score, they can click the 'finish' button to reveal the winner.
