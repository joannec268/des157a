Design Comp Version 2

After getting feedback from class critique, I made a new iteration on my Game On! design. Figure 1 shows the screen when the game has not been started.

As shown in Figures 2 and 3, the main changes I made are enhancing the feedback for whose player's turn it is by adding an arrow sign on top of the player. I plan to animate to make the arrow move up and down to accentuate the feedback when I begin coding. Additionally, I redesigned the player's score display by having a background.

Other minor changes in this iteration include padding, spacing, font size, and button size.

As users add more cards, the size of the cards will shrink accordingly to the constrained space in the middle as shown in Figure 4.
